Instructions

  1. First, draw your polygon by clicking in the vertices in clockwise order.
  2. When you have selected the last vertex, press Enter.
  3. Then a message will appear telling you to introduce two points to route between. You have to select two points (first the origin, shown in red, and then the destination, shown in green) inside the polygon and press the key ‘s’ (Start).
  4. You will see the triangulation in grey and the sufficient beacons to route between every pair of points will appear successively in red.
  5. Then, after a message prevents you, you will see the particular beacons to route between the two points you have introduced, in blue.
